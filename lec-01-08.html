🎯 Exam focus: Focus on research designs, data types, statistics, framework, citations, and research process.
  • Null hypothesis means no relationship or no significant difference between variables.
  • Quantitative uses numerical data; qualitative uses labels, names, narratives, and descriptions.
  • Descriptive statistics describes data; inferential statistics uses sample data to make predictions about a population.
  • Mean, median, and mode are basic measures commonly tested.
  • Hypothesis is the expected outcome or testable prediction of a study.
